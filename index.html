Terms and Conditions

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing or using Cruzio's services ("Service"), you agree to be bound by these Terms and Conditions ("Terms"). If you do not agree with any part of these terms, you may not use our services.

2. Eligibility

To rent a vehicle from Cruzio, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Be at least 21 years of age.
  • Hold a valid driving license for at least 1 year.
  • Provide valid government-issued ID proof (Aadhar, PAN, or Passport).
  • Have a valid credit/debit card for payment.

3. Booking and Payment

All bookings must be made through our website or mobile app. Payment is required at the time of booking. We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and UPI payments. Prices are inclusive of all taxes unless stated otherwise.

4. Vehicle Usage

The rented vehicle may only be used for lawful purposes within the territorial limits of India. The following activities are strictly prohibited:

  • Sub-renting the vehicle to third parties.
  • Using the vehicle for racing or competitive events.
  • Transporting illegal substances or contraband.
  • Towing or pushing other vehicles.
  • Driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs.

5. Fuel Policy

Vehicles are provided with a full tank of fuel and must be returned with a full tank. If the vehicle is returned with less fuel, refueling charges plus a service fee will apply.

6. Damage and Insurance

All our vehicles are covered by comprehensive insurance. In case of an accident, the renter is liable for a maximum of Rs. 10,000 as damages. For damages exceeding this amount, insurance will cover the costs subject to policy terms and conditions.

7. Modification of Terms

Cruzio reserves the right to modify these terms at any time. Continued use of our services after modifications constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

Privacy Policy

We collect the following types of information to provide you with a seamless rental experience:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and driving license details.
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details (stored securely with PCI-compliant processors).
  • Location Data: GPS data during the rental period for vehicle tracking and safety.
  • Usage Data: Browsing history, booking preferences, and app usage patterns.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • Processing bookings and payments securely.
  • Verifying identity and driving credentials.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with trusted third parties under the following circumstances:

  • Service providers who assist in our operations (e.g., maintenance, cleaning).

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, firewalls, and secure data centers to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also opt-out of marketing communications at any time. For any privacy-related inquiries, contact us at privacy@cruzio.in.

Rental Guidelines & Policies

Vehicle Usage Policy

Thank you for choosing Cruzio. To ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience for all guests, please adhere to the following guidelines:

Do's

  • Carry a valid driving license and booking documents at pickup.
  • Inspect the vehicle at pickup and inform us immediately if you notice any issue.
  • Drive responsibly and follow all traffic rules.
  • Return the vehicle with the same fuel level as at pickup.
  • Maintain cleanliness inside the vehicle.
  • Inform us immediately in case of any incident or breakdown.
  • Return the vehicle with the FASTag balance maintained at the same amount as at pickup, or settle toll deductions at return.

Don'ts

  • Allow unauthorized persons to drive the vehicle.
  • Engage in rash driving, racing, or off-road usage.
  • Smoke or drink inside the vehicle.
  • Drive through waterlogged or extreme terrain areas.
  • Overload beyond permitted passenger capacity.
  • Carry items or pets that may damage interiors unless informed in advance.
  • Exceed 100 km/h speed. Exceeding this limit may attract a penalty.

Usage Limit

Daily usage limit: 350 km per day. Additional usage will be charged at ₹10 per km beyond the limit.
